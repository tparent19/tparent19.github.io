I am a Business Marketing major at The University of Maine in my second year. I started out my freshman year majoring in civil engineering but decided that business was a better fit for me. One of the many things I love about Umaine is how helpful my advisors were, they made my transition between major very easy and kept me on the right track with my classes. I am from Maine so my transition into college wasnt very difficult, but all the students and staff here are friendly and make you feel at home.
